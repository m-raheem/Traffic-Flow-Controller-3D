About Us

Traffic congestion is a massive problem in big cities. There are number of vehicles stuck in congestion for several hours. One reason beyond the traffic congestion is construction/maintenance at roadside due to which vehicles either lower their speed or reroute them to other roads and massive rerouting on a single route result in congestion. Second reason is inefficient time division at signals that result in congestion. Many signals are working at uniform manner for all the roads even if the road is empty signal will wait for that time and then move on to the next road that results inefficient time division.

Fortunately, this issue can be addressed using machine learning to collect data on traffic congestion and a visualization of traffic flow in a 3d model so road authorities can have a visual representation of traffic flow to address any event accordingly. Representing the total flow at real time can be very efficient for addressing many issues including congestion, privileged moment, and constructions/maintenance. Providing the efficient 3D model can be used to Train devices via machine learning for real world scenario
